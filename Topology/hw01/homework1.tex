		\[
		\mathcal{T}_1 = \{ U \subseteq X : U = \emptyset \text{ or } X \setminus U \text{ is finite} \}
		\]
		is a topology on \( X \), called the \textbf{finite complement topology}.
		
		\item[(b)] Show that
		\[
		\mathcal{T}_2 = \{ U \subseteq X : U = \emptyset \text{ or } X \setminus U \text{ is countable} \}
		\]
		is a topology on \( X \), called the \textbf{countable complement topology}.
